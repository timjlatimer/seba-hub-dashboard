Seba Hub Occupancy Engine

A live preview for the team · Seba Beach, AB
The goal: fill Seba Hub to 85% occupancy this season. The idea in one line — instead of buying ads to interrupt people, we listen for people already looking for a getaway and reach out warmly. AI does the heavy lifting (find them, draft the message); a person only steps in for the part that needs a human. Nothing is ever sent without one of us approving it.
Track A · Midweek Groups. We find the one person organizing a group (church, club, league) — they book midweek on purpose, and one yes = 10–50 beds.

Track B · Weekend Individuals. People wanting a weekend away get a friendly reply + a self-book link (sebastays.com). They book themselves — nearly hands-free.
